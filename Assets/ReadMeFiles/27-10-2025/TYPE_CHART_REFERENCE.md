## üéÆ Starter Matchups (with Bone-type moves)

All starters use Bone-type moves at Level 1:

**Skeleton (Bone type) using Slash (Bone move):**
- vs Zombie (Plague): 1.0x neutral
- vs Ghoul (Feral): 1.0x neutral
- **Winner based on stats**

**Zombie (Plague type) using Tackle (Bone move):**
- vs Skeleton (Bone): 1.0x neutral  
- vs Ghoul (Feral): 1.0x neutral
- **Winner based on stats**

**Ghoul (Feral type) using Claw (Bone move):**
- vs Skeleton (Bone): 1.0x neutral
- vs Zombie (Plague): 1.0x neutral
- **Winner based on stats**

**Result:** All matchups are stat-based until they learn typed moves!

## üìà Level 5+ Matchups (with typed moves)

## üí° Design Philosophy

**Why these matchups?**

1. **Bone as Normal:** Predictable baseline, can't hit spirits (teaches immunity)
2. **Plague beats physical:** Disease corrupts bones and flesh
3. **Feral beats Plague:** Predators resist infection
4. **Spirit vs Physical:** Ghost immunity creates tactical puzzle
5. **Fire purifies:** Thematic counter to organic undead
6. **Holy struggles vs soulless:** Bones resist divine magic

**Balance Goals:**
- No type is useless (all have 2+ good matchups)
- Immunities create interesting decisions (Bone can't hit Spirit)
- Early game mostly Bone enemies (fair difficulty curve)
- Type advantages matter but don't guarantee wins (1.5x not 2x)
